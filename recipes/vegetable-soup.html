Vegetable Soup

A Delicious Vegetable soup

Vegetable soup is one of the many Nigerian native delicious soup

Ingredients

  • Big bowl Water-leaves
  • Big bowl ugu-leaves(fluted pumpkin)
  • Roasted mackerel
  • 1KG or 35 Ounces of Goat meat
  • 1 cup crayfish
  • 6 scotch bonnet peppers
  • 400ml Palm oil
  • 2 Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons Ground Ofor

Steps

  1. Wash fluted pumpkin leaves before slicing it. And wash the water-leave after slicing it
  2. Slice the Water-leaves and fluted pumpkin with a sharp knife
  3. Wash the roasted fish, season the goat meat and boil
  4. While the meat is boiling add the washed roasted fish inside
  5. Add 400ml palm oil inside the boiling meat, once the water has completely dried
  6. Add the vegetables
  7. Add the pounded crayfish and peppers
  8. Add the ugu-leaves(fluted pumpkin) at this point
